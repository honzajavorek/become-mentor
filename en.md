- [original slides](https://speakerdeck.com/ossanna16/keynote-pycon-cz-2017-be-come-a-mentor-help-others-succeed)
- [video recording](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=motIB96FI48&t=14930s)

<small>The idea of this transcript is heavily inspired by [Ana Balica](https://twitter.com/anabalica)'s transcript of her [Humanizing among coders](https://ana-balica.github.io/2017/05/28/humanizing-among-coders/) keynote for [PyCon CZ 2016](https://cz.pycon.org/2016/).</small>
